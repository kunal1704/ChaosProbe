# Abstract Draft

ChaosProbe is a neurochaos-inspired framework for analyzing transformer
embeddings through trajectory-derived descriptors. Rather than compressing an
embedding vector into a single summary, ChaosProbe preserves a structured
descriptor tensor with separate channels for threshold-time symbolic behavior,
energy, time-to-match, and entropy. This design is motivated by the idea that
coordinate-wise dynamical summaries may reveal representation geometry that is
not apparent from standard projection-based analyses. The project is still at
the repository-skeleton stage, and the first experiment will focus on
descriptor-baseline comparisons and methodology validation before any broader
claims are considered.
